My name is Henley. I live and study in Sydney. I graduated from the University of NSW in late 2023 with a Bachelor of Architectural Studies and began my Master of Architecture at the University of Sydney in the begining of 2025. Since November 2023, I have been working with Ozaki Studio in Wollongong. During this time, I have contributed to a range of projects, including the refurbishment of an office building in Sydney, a terrace house renovation in Sydney, and a family home in Sydney's Northern Beaches. I have also been involved in several design competitions, including NOW NOW: Beyond the Classroom and NOW NOW: Our Homes, the latter receiving an Honourable Mention. This website is a platform to display my full work through university studio and work with Ozaki Studio.
